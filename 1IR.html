Fit Suggestions

  • Measure your feet in the afternoon, as feet tend to swell during the day.
  • Make sure to measure both feet, as one foot might be slightly larger than the other.
  • When trying on shoes, wear the type of socks you plan to wear with the shoes.
  • Leave about a thumb's width of space between your longest toe and the end of the shoe.
  • Walk around in the shoes to ensure they're comfortable and provide proper support.

Regional Differences

Keep in mind that sizes may vary slightly between brands and regions. When in doubt, it's best to refer to the brand's specific size chart or measure your feet in centimeters/inches for more accurate sizing.
